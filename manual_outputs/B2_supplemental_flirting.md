## Grammar Notes

**Target structures used in this story:**

- **Estar a + Infinitivo (present continuous)**: *"a rir nervosamente e a desviar o olhar"* — European Portuguese uses *estar a + infinitivo* for actions in progress, where Brazilian Portuguese would use *estar + gerúndio*

- **Pretérito Imperfeito (habitual past)**: *"que me beijava consistentemente no canto da boca ao cumprimentar-me"* — the imperfect tense expresses repeated or habitual past actions, equivalent to "used to" or "would" in English

- **Infinitivo Pessoal**: *"antes disso acontecer"* — a uniquely Portuguese construction where the infinitive is inflected for person; here the uninflected form is used with an implicit subject

- **Contração em + artigo definido**: *"perguntei à minha amiga"* — *a + a = à*; similarly *em + o = no*, *em + a = na*, used throughout for location and direction

- **Conjuntivo / Subjuntivo (present)**: *"Se não fores nenhuma das coisas"* — the subjunctive is triggered after *se* in hypothetical conditions, a key structure in the Champions League guide

- **Pronome de complemento indireto**: *"disse-me", "manda-lhe mensagem", "pede-lhe o número"* — clitic pronouns attach to the verb in EP and express indirect objects (*me* = to me, *lhe* = to her/him)
